Lab News and Updates

  • April 2025: Dr. Sharma's lab website goes live showcasing research, publications, and team updates.
  • February 2025: Dr. Sharma receives the HERA Ovarian Cancer Foundation grant for advancing molecular radiotherapy research.
  • October 2024: Submitted NIH Trailblazer Award grant titled "Development of an In Vivo Stable Fluorosulfate Group for Rapid and Late-Stage F-18 Labeling".
  • October 2024: Dr. Sharma's team receives the Colorectal Cancer Research Center of Excellence Pilot Award.
  • October 2024: Awarded the ERF NET RF Research Grant to support nuclear medicine innovation.
  • September 2024: Manuscript titled "An EphA2-targeted theranostic for enhancing PDAC treatment" prepared and submitted.
  • 2023: Key publications on peptide radiochemistry and imaging agents for PD-L1 and CD38 appear in leading journals.
